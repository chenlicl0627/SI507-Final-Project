Employee Reviews for Big Tech Companies

Introduction

On this website you will find some simple data analysis based on over 67k employee reviews for six big technology companies, including Google, Amazon, Apple, Microsoft Facebook and Netflix, between 2008 and 2018 on Glassdoor.
I acquired the dataset from kaggle.com which you can see HERE

What you can do on this website

In addition to the homepage, there are another pages on this website:

  • Reviewing the most frequent word(s) in either the pros or cons reviews of each company.

  • Reviewing the average work-life balance rating for each company.
